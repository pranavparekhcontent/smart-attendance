Frequently Asked Questions

Is Smart Attendance really free?

Yes, Smart Attendance is completely free for all teachers, professors, and educational institutions. There are no hidden charges, subscription fees, or premium tiers. The app is funded through minimal non-intrusive advertisements.

Does it work without internet?

Absolutely. Smart Attendance is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that works fully offline once installed. Attendance data is saved locally and automatically syncs to Google Sheets when you reconnect to the internet.

Which devices are supported?

Smart Attendance works on all modern devices — Android phones and tablets (Chrome), iPhones and iPads (Safari), Windows laptops and desktops (Chrome/Edge), and macOS computers (Chrome/Safari). Install it directly from your browser.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 29, 2026

Smart Attendance ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This privacy statement explains how our application processes academic and user data.

1. Data Processing and Storage

Smart Attendance is a progressive client-side application. We do not maintain any central servers or databases to store student names, registration logs, or roll-call sheets. All data stays strictly in:

  • Your local device storage (IndexedDB/Local Storage) for offline app usage.
  • Your authorized target Google Spreadsheets sheets.

2. Third-Party Integrations

The application interacts directly with standard Google Web APIs (Google Apps Script) to sync spreadsheet files. These interactions are governed entirely by your own Google Account agreements and settings.

3. Device Permissions

This application does not request access to device location services, contact records, local device cameras, or files outside the browser sandbox context.

4. Ad Networks and Analytics

We may display advertisements provided by third-party ad networks (like Monetag or Adsterra) to fund hosting and app upkeep. These ad agencies may use small tracking cookies to serve relevant advertisements to users.

5. Contact Details

For any questions regarding product security, licensing, or active license key deletions, contact the developer at: pranavparekhcontent@gmail.com.
