In this article, we will outline the benefits of each payment method, allowing you to select the one that best suits your needs.

What payment methods are available in Monetag?

We understand how vital the on-time, correct, and transparent payments are for our customers, and that is why we offer a wide range of payment options for our Publishers, including the following ones:

  • Cryptocurrencies

  • PayPal

  • Skrill

  • Webmoney

  • Payoneer Account

  • WIRE/Online Banking Wire/Revolut

Please note that the list of payment methods available for a particular account depends on the country of residence.

What are the minimum payouts?

Minimum payouts (the minimum amount from which a payment can be generated) vary for different payment methods:

Cryptocurrencies - $100

PayPal - $5

Skrill - $5 (please note that the payments are processed in Euros)
​Webmoney - $5
​Payoneer Account - $20/$30 (depends on the country)

WIRE/Online Banking Wire/Revolut - $500 (money transfers below $1,000 are charged with a bank fee of $50)

The earnings are transferred to the next payment cycle if the available balance on the payment date is lower than the minimum withdrawal amount.

What is the fee?

Please note that third-party services may charge you a fee to receive payments from us. You will have to visit their websites for your country to review the fees associated with receiving payments. Please rest assured that Monetag does not take any commissions or fees.

How to choose a payment method

Let’s take a closer look at how to select a payment method for your account. To do so, you should go to the Payment Methods tab at the top right corner of your personal account and choose from the list of available options.

Another option to set the payment is to click on the payment tab in your account dashboard.
​


Choose a method by which you would like to receive money and fill in the required fields.

If you work with Payoneer, remember to link your Payoneer account to Monetag. For more information on how to do this, please refer to the manual.

Follow through on each step to ensure that your payment method and required information have been saved properly. Your payment details must be accurate to ensure the timely receipt of payments.

Once the method is attached, you can also change the minimum withdrawal amount. This can be done on the "Payment methods" section on the Monetag dashboard once you set a default payment method:
​

Below is a review of the above-mentioned payment methods.

Cryptocurrencies

You may receive earnings in Bitcoin, USDT TRC, or USDT ERC through a licensed payment service provider such as CoinsPaid or similar platforms.

The fees are applicable and may vary depending on the crypto exchange platform.

In order to add this payment method to your Monetag account, you should:

  • Go to the "Payment Methods" tab

  • Choose Cryptocurrencies from the menu
    ​

  • Choose a currency type

  • Enter your wallet ID

  • Click on the “Save” button.


PayPal

PayPal is one of the world's largest Internet payment companies, which allows users to process online money payments. PayPal has become a highly popular payment method, as it requires only your email address to initiate the payment process. You will receive an email with a link connecting your PayPal e-wallet to your Monetag account. This payment method is very swift and is available in USD.

  • Go to the "Payment Methods" tab

  • Choose PayPal from the dropdown menu and enter your PayPal email address.
    ​

  • The verification link will be sent to your email address.

  • Click on the link in the email to confirm the changes, and you’ll be redirected to your account.

*** Please note that after you add PayPal as a new payment method and set it as the default, we will send a test withdrawal of $5 to your account. It is not sent immediately after a PayPal account is added to your Monetag account. Usually, it is sent during the account verification process (if the account has not yet been verified). This is necessary so that we can verify the payment method for the withdrawal.


Skrill

It is a platform designed for individuals new to money transfers and digital wallets. Skrill is a safe, convenient e-commerce and money transfer service that allows you to withdraw your earnings in EUR.

How do I set this payment method?

  • Go to the "Payment Methods" tab

  • Choose Skrill from the dropdown menu

  • Add the email address linked to your Skrill account and hit the “Save” button.
    ​

  • The verification link will be sent to your email address.

  • Click on the link in the email to confirm the changes, and you’ll be redirected to your account.

If you want to receive your next payout through Skrill, remember to set it as your default payment method.
​
​Please note that you may need to pass the KYC in order to start receiving payments via Skrill.

Additionally, you will need to remove authorization limits so that we can send you payments.


Webmoney

Transactions are performed with ease when you use this method of payment.

You simply register an account at www.wmtransfer.com and then create the relevant purse to make transactions in your desired currency. Please note that you should create a WMZ purse for USD.

Once you have your Webmoney purse details, you can add them to your profile as a payment method.

  • Go to the "Payment Methods" tab

  • Choose Webmoney from the dropdown menu

  • Fill in your Webmoney account details and the payment account owner's personal info (please check that your e-wallet is verified)
    ​

  • Choose a currency (WMZ or WMT)

  • Click on the “Save” button


Payoneer Account

To receive your payments via Payoneer, you will need to sign up for a Payoneer account or link your existing Payoneer Account to your Monetag account.

  • Go to the "Payment Methods" tab

  • Choose Payoneer from the dropdown menu

WIRE/Online Banking Wire/Revolut

A wire transfer is an electronic transfer of money. Wire transfers are popular because the money moves very fast. Usually, your bank will receive the funds within 3-4 business days. However, be informed that there are some factors that could result in a delay of a wire transfer: national and local bank holidays, delays due to an intermediary bank, or other local conditions. Some transfers sent to certain countries may experience delays of up to 2 weeks. If a wire transfer is taking too long, we can provide you with a swift solution. You can also ask your bank for information about your transfer status using the transaction number.

Please be reminded that wire transfers are the only payment method that requires a minimum threshold of $500 USD. Additionally, a commission of $ 50 is charged for all wire transfers below $1,000 USD.

Wire transfers are available in EUR or USD, which you can choose in the Profile section when setting up your payment method.

  • Go to the "Payment Methods" tab in the Monetag dashboard.

  • Choose Wire from the dropdown menu.

You will have to complete the following information:

  • Bank name - your bank name;

  • Bank address - your bank address;

  • Account number - your account number;

  • IBAN - the international standard for identifying international bank accounts across national borders;

  • SWIFT - a unique identification code that may be required by some banks for the completion of international wire transfers;

  • Comments - just a message to the bank.

Please note that not all countries are supported for wire transfers. Our support team is available 24/7 to provide guidance as needed.

​
​Please note that in some cases, KYC verification might be required.
